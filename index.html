About GetYoked

GetYoked is an addictive roguelike deckbuilder game where you take on the role of an aspiring bodybuilder. Your goal is to build the perfect physique by strategically playing workout cards, managing fatigue, and making smart decisions about your training regimen.

In GetYoked, you'll face increasingly difficult competitions that require specific muscle development. Will you become a balanced bodybuilder with proportional development, or will you specialize in certain muscle groups to create a unique physique?

The game features intuitive controls, engaging gameplay mechanics, and a progression system that keeps you coming back for more. With each run, you'll learn new strategies and unlock new cards to help you on your journey to becoming the ultimate bodybuilding champion.

How to Play

  1. Build Your Deck: Collect workout cards, supplements, and rest strategies to create your perfect training regimen.
  2. Manage Fatigue: Balance intense workouts with proper rest to avoid injury and maximize gains.
  3. Win Competitions: Meet the minimum muscle requirements for each competition to progress through the game.
  4. Strategic Choices: Decide which muscle groups to prioritize based on competition requirements and your personal strategy.
  5. Unlock New Cards: As you progress, you'll unlock new workout cards and supplements to enhance your training options.
